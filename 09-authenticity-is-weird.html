‚ú® Authenticity is Weird

The vulnerably exposed, heart cracked wide open, wow-I-actually-said-that-out-loud kind of way

"Embracing your truest self is not just a practice or statement, but a way of life. It is your superpower - it is your MAGIC wand. Wield it with pride and purpose."

The deeper I go into this journey, the weirder authenticity actually feels. Not in a bad way, but in that "vulnerably exposed, heart cracked wide open, wow-I-actually-said-that-out-loud" kind of way.

Speaking your truth in any volume is a powerful energetic act that aligns your inner world with your outer expression. It activates your throat chakra, which governs communication, authenticity, and self-expression. When this energy center is open and balanced, you can express yourself clearly, confidently, and with integrity.

The Energetics of Speaking Your Truth

When you speak your truth, you create alignment between your heart and your voice. When you speak from a place of authenticity, your heart (love, intuition) and your throat (expression) are in harmony. This creates a flow of high vibrational energy that strengthens your sense of self.

Holding back your truth creates energetic congestion, often manifesting as tension in the throat, chest, or jaw. Speaking openly allows stagnant energy to release, leading to a sense of relief and empowerment.

Truth carries a very high frequency and vibration that resonates outward. When you express yourself honestly and authentically, you naturally attract people and opportunities that align with your true essence - while simultaneously repelling anything that is not meant for you.

"That part can be a little wild at first, because sometimes what falls away isn't what you expected to lose. But in time, you'll realize that it made room for something more aligned."

The Physical Symptoms of Suppression

When you suppress your truth, energy stagnates. This often leads to frustration, resentment, or even physical symptoms such as:

A lump in the throat

Chronic sore throat

Thyroid imbalances

Jaw tension and TMJ

Neck and shoulder tightness

When you express yourself from a place of love and wisdom, you activate the flow of divine energy, strengthening your own intuition and personal power.

Here's the Weird Part

Sometimes authenticity feels isolating, especially when you're the only one choosing it in a room full of masks. It's vulnerable. It's raw. It's real. That's exactly why it's so powerful.

Since embracing my fullest self and becoming more visible - especially on social media - I've lost friends. I've lost some family, too. I've been unfollowed, judged, misunderstood, and side-eyed. But I've also never felt more like ME.

Some people seem to like you more when you're falling apart quietly in the corner than when you rise up, take the mic, and start telling the truth. That truth might be messy. It might be weird. It might involve a disco ball hat you wore on a soul-revealing trip to Vegas with one of your best friends, crying in a bathroom and then laughing so hard it became medicine.

"I didn't come here to be palatable. I came here to love. To share. To heal. I'm not stopping."

Real is Rare

You don't have to understand every part of me to feel the energy I bring. That's the thing about authenticity - it's not always neat. But it's real. Real is rare.

This is your reminder that the more real you are, the more magnetic you become. Even if it's weird. Especially if it's weird. That weirdness? That's your soul showing.

Let it shine.

What I've Learned

It's not my job to fix what I didn't break.

I don't need to chase people who walk away.

I won't shrink myself to make others comfortable.

If it's not reciprocal, it's not aligned.

If it costs my peace, it's too expensive.

If it makes me question my worth, it's not welcome in my life.

I was never too much. I was never the problem. I was a mirror. What I reflect now is light, softness, joy, and strength. Not just for me. For all of us.

Because this path was never meant to be walked alone. now - we shine.

"Your voice is your healing tool - not just for yourself, but for others. When you speak with integrity, you encourage others to do the same, creating an energetic ripple effect of authenticity and connection."
