The Law of Authenticity

Authenticity is one of the Laws of the Universe that is now making its way into the collective consciousness more than ever before. The Law of Authenticity states that the most valuable gift you have to offer is yourself. It is vibrationally more powerful than LOVE.

This law implies that no matter what skills, knowledge, or talents you have, they become exponentially more powerful when combined with your sincere, real presence. This includes the open expression of your truest authentic emotions and thoughts.

People can sense when someone is being real versus when they are putting on a persona, mask, or a front. If you are saying and doing one thing while feeling and thinking another, it creates dissonance and energetic entanglements.

Energetic Entanglements

Energetic entanglements refer to the deep, often unseen connections we form with people, places, and experiences on an energetic or spiritual level. These entanglements can be positive - bringing love and harmony - or negative, creating emotional drain, confusion, or even physical discomfort.

We may even unconsciously take on the pain patterns or physical issues of those around us.

"When we begin a journey of healing, one of the most important questions to ask is - Is this even mine?"

When we share strong emotions with someone (pain, loss, excitement, love, anger, grief, joy), our energy intertwines with theirs. Intimacy, touch, or even prolonged time in someone's presence can also create these energetic imprints. Thinking about someone constantly or having unresolved emotions toward them can create an ongoing energetic link.

Not only can this deplete us - it can keep us from showing up as our truest selves. They are not YOU. They are not YOURS. they are most likely not in alignment with how you want to show up or who you want to BE.

Signs of Energetic Entanglements

Feeling the emotions, thoughts, or energy of others - even when they're not around

Repeated thoughts or dreams about someone from your past - even when you try to move on

Exhaustion, feeling drained, or negative emotions after interacting with certain people

Anxiety or feeling "stuck" that you can't explain

Physical discomfort in certain locations or around certain objects

Clairsentience: The Gift of Clear Feeling

Clairsentience is the intuitive ability to perceive energy, emotions, and sensations beyond the physical senses. The word itself comes from French, meaning "clear feeling." It is one of the primary "clair" abilities, alongside clairvoyance (clear seeing), clairaudience (clear hearing), and claircognizance (clear knowing).

Someone with clairsentience can pick up on the emotions, energy, or even physical sensations of people, places, or situations - without any obvious external clues.

Suddenly experiencing sadness, anxiety, or joy that isn't yours

Sensing that physical locations feel dark, heavy, warm, or light due to residual energy

Gut feelings, chills, tingles, or physical discomfort near certain people or objects

Receiving messages through bodily sensations that guide you toward or away from choices

Instinctively "knowing" when someone is lying or when something is off

"While empathic awareness and the ability to feel another person's pain, excitement, or exhaustion can be a beautiful gift that healers encompass - it's not actually YOU. It is very important to cultivate practices to differentiate these energies."

Clearing Your Energy

When I work with clients, we set an intention to clear, heal, release, and repair anything and everything that no longer serves - anything that isn't ours - and anything of a lower frequency or vibration than our highest intentions for ourselves and our highest potential, which includes our highest expressions of authenticity.

Empathy and clairsentience are beautiful and powerful gifts that allow access to deep connection and insight. They can be overwhelming at times - but learning to manage and trust your feelings, while strengthening your true authentic self, can support you in utilizing these gifts as powerful tools for guidance and healing - for yourself and for others.

"When you embrace all that YOU are, you become more of who you might BE."
