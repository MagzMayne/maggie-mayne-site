Your words are your wand

"Setting intentions isn't just about hoping or wishing for something to magically appear. It's a conscious, focused act of directing your energy and will toward a specific outcome. It's how you begin telling the universe what you're ready for - and how you begin telling yourself the truth about what you actually want."

In every client session, we focus on intentions. We begin with the intentions for the session itself, and then we zoom out and look at what I like to call our "all-the-time" intentions - the deeper soul desires we carry with us day in and day out.

This is one of the most important elements within your power on your healing journey.

Intention is the compass guiding your healing process, aligning your body, mind, emotions, and spirit toward a desired state of feeling and being. just like a compass, it doesn't always dictate the exact path - it simply keeps you moving in the right direction.

The Power of Clarity

This isn't about controlling or forcing a particular outcome. It's about creating fertile ground where healing can flourish. It's about choosing alignment over resistance. Surrender over forcing. Clarity over confusion.

What are your current challenges?

What's asking to be healed, released, or reclaimed?

What would it feel like to be on the other side of that?

How will you know when your intention has manifested?

The more specific you are, the easier it becomes to track your progress and adjust your course. Instead of "I want to be happy," we dig deeper - what does happiness mean to you? What does it look like? Feel like? What does it do for your daily life?
